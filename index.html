FAQ

Common Questions

What documents do I need to start the process? +
You'll need your academic transcripts, citizenship document, passport, English language test scores (IELTS/TOEFL), and a recent CV. Our counselors will guide you on any additional country-specific requirements during your free consultation.
How early should I start the application process? +
We recommend starting at least 12–18 months before your intended intake. This allows ample time for test preparation, shortlisting, application, offer, and visa processing without rushing.
What is your consultation fee? +
Your first consultation is completely free. Our service packages are structured based on your destination and level of support needed. We believe in transparent pricing with no hidden charges — everything is explained upfront.
Do you guarantee visa approval? +
No agency can legally guarantee visa approval as the decision rests with the embassy. However, our 98% success rate speaks to our thorough preparation, documentation, and interview coaching that significantly maximises your chances.
Can you help with part-time work opportunities abroad? +
Absolutely. We provide information on work rights for international students in each country (e.g., 20 hrs/week in Australia and UK), and connect you with our alumni network who can share practical job-hunting tips upon arrival.
